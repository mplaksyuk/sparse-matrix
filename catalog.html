The period of 1960-1980 is considered to be a turning point in architecture in its content: cardinal updates of forms and constructions, refusal of decoration and nationalization, functional approach. Following the principles that the architects of the twentieth century adopted for themselves, the designer of the brand created six images inspired by the architecture of Kyiv. Recognizable objects of the native city were chosen: the library named after V.I.Vernadsky, Hotel «Salut», Hotel «Mir», «Memorial Park», the building of the Faculty of Physics of KNU named after T. Shevchenko, UkrINTEI, known by townsfolk as the «Plate».
By this work, the PODYH brand aimed to draw public attention to the uniqueness and beauty of architecture, which is currently in an abandoned state and needs proper restoration. Wanting to influence the situation, the team decided to give part of the proceeds from the sale of the collection to restore the wall of the «Memorial Park», which was planned as the largest bas-relief in Europe. Its fate is quite tragic: for «inconsistency» with the ideas of socialism, this monument was not finished: already at the final stage of work in 1982, it was filled with concrete.
